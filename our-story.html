Who We Are

Where Purpose
Meets Craft

ECORA was built on a simple idea: that everyday objects can be designed with intention, made with care, and used without waste.

The Beginning

A brand born from frustration with the ordinary.

ECORA started as a response to a market saturated with disposable, forgettable branded merchandise — products that are received, used once, and discarded. The founders believed that corporate gifting could mean something more.

Every object ECORA creates is designed to serve a real, ongoing purpose — to be kept, used, and valued. This is not about aesthetics alone, but about objects that earn a place in daily life.

What Drives Us

Three principles that shape everything

01

Restraint

We resist complexity for its own sake. Every element in an Ecora product is there because it earns its place — nothing more, nothing less.

02

Responsibility

From material selection to end of life, we take ownership of the impact our products have — on people, communities, and the environment.

03

Clarity

Clear purpose. Clear materials. Clear communication. We believe the best products speak plainly — and so does the brand behind them.

Our Journey

From idea to impact

1

The Founding Problem (2019)

Frustrated by a market full of throwaway branded merchandise, the founding team asked: what if corporate gifting meant something lasting?

2

First Collection (2020)

Ecora launched with three products: a canvas tote, a bamboo notebook, and a seed-paper card set. All zero plastic packaging. All still in the catalogue today.

3

Corporate Programme (2022)

Partnerships with forward-thinking brands across the GCC led to a dedicated corporate giftset programme — curated, branded, and built to last.

4

500+ Brands Served (Today)

Over 50,000 products delivered. Zero plastic in packaging. A growing community of brands choosing responsible gifting.
